Resources and Support

Homepage: https://www.gnuhealth.org

Mastodon: For regular news updates we suggest that you follow GNU Health on Mastodon at : https://mastodon.social/@gnuhealth

Matrix: https://matrix.to/#/#gnuhealth-space:matrix.org

Mailing lists: There are several GNUHealth mailing lists at GNU Savannah. To subscribe or view the archives of these mailing lists, please visit https://savannah.gnu.org/mail/?group=health The most active and relevant are “health” for general discussions and “health-dev” for developers. Please note that you need to suscribe. Emails that are from non-members are automatically discarded.

Training and support for elegible projects

GNU Solidario can provide training and implementation support to elegible institutions.

In order to be elegible, the following requirements must be met:

  • An introduction of the current needs

  • The project will use free/Libre software

  • There will be a local designated person that will be in charge of the project and the know-how transfer to the rest of the community.This person must be committed to be from the beginning of the project until two years after its completion.

Please send your project support request to: info@gnuhealth.org
